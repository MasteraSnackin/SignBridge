## Executive Summary

### What is SignBridge?

**SignBridge** is a revolutionary mobile application that provides **real-time, fully offline, bidirectional translation** between American Sign Language (ASL) and spoken English. Built with cutting-edge on-device AI, it enables seamless communication between deaf and hearing individuals without requiring internet connectivity.
